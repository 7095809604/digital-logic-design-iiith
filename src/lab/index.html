  • Introduction

    Welcome to the Digital Logic Design Lab. Here you can make all the basic digital circuit of your choice and simulate it .

    Target Audience

    • Students doing courses on Digital Logic Circuit will be benifited
    • Some basic prior knowledge of the logic gates are required

    Relevant Courses

    All the courses related to the Digital Logical circuit are relevant . For ex:- Digital Logical Processing

    Objectives

    • To allow user to build and understand the working of digital Logic Circuit
    • User should be also be able to save its own circuit and reuse it.
    • User should be understand working of circuit in presence of time variant input pulses
    • Starting from beginner level to advance level , all the important concepts related to DLD should be covered.
